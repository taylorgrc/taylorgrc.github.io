Introduction

Pas de Chat

Pas de Chat is an online fashion store that sells crocheted garments.

Target audience

The target audience is crochet lovers, fashion fanatics, and general clothing shoppers.

Users of this site might want to purchase items or get crochet and clothing inspiration. Crochet lovers might visit the site to get pattern inspiration and general fashion lovers and shoppers would visit the site to purchase one-of-a-kind clothes and accessories and support a local artist.

Why Crochet?

Crocheting is a method of slow-fashion that is an eco-friendly alternative to shopping fast fashion. Handmade garments means recycled materials, sustainably sourced materials, and an honset source.

About Us

Inspired by the delicate qualities of ballet attire, we strive to create original pieces that everyone will feel comfortable wearing.

We are a garment company located in San Luis Obispo. Our clothing and accessories are hand-crocheted and made to order. We use sustainably sourced and recycled yarn when possible.
